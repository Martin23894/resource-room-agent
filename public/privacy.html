Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Placeholder notice. This is a draft template intended to outline our approach to teacher data. Before going to production, please review it with a legal advisor familiar with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Who we are

The Resource Room ("we", "us") is a teaching-resource generator for South African Grades 4–7. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it and how we protect it.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address, your display name, your school, your role and the grades and subjects you teach (only what you choose to provide).
  • Authentication data — magic-link tokens and session cookies, used only to keep you signed in.
  • Usage data — the resources you generate (grade, term, subject, topic, resource type) so we can show you your history and improve the product.
  • Technical data — IP address and request metadata, for security and rate-limiting.

We do not collect learner data, payment card details (those are handled directly by Stripe if you subscribe), or any sensitive personal information.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the service — sign you in, generate resources, save your settings and history.
  • To send transactional emails (sign-in links, account notifications).
  • To prevent abuse — rate limiting and security monitoring.
  • To improve the product — aggregated, de-identified usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers.

4. How we protect your information

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS).
  • Session cookies are HMAC-signed and HTTP-only.
  • Passwords are not stored — we use passwordless magic-link sign-in.
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorised operators on a need-to-know basis.

5. Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can update your profile from your account page. To delete your account, open the Account modal in the app and use the "Delete my account" button in the Danger zone — your profile, settings, history and sign-in sessions are removed from our database immediately.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, settings, history and sign-in sessions are removed from our database immediately. We may retain minimal billing records for legal and tax purposes (these are kept by our payment processor, not us).

7. Third-party services

  • Anthropic — generation requests are sent to the Claude API. We do not send your name, email, or any other personal information in those requests.
  • Resend — used to deliver sign-in emails.
  • Stripe — used to process subscription payments (if you choose to subscribe).
  • Railway — used to host the application.

8. Cookies

We use a single first-party cookie (rr_session) to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests? Email hello@theresourceroom.co.za.

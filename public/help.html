Getting started

How do I create my first resource?
  1. Sign in to the app.
  2. In the sidebar, pick a Grade (4–7), Term (1–4), and Subject.
  3. Pick a Topic and a Resource type (Worksheet, Test, Lesson…).
  4. Set the language, marks and difficulty, then click Generate resource pack.

You'll get an editable Word document (and a PowerPoint deck for Lessons) you can download, print or share.

Which grades and subjects are supported?

We currently support Grades 4–7 across all CAPS subjects taught in those grades — Mathematics, English HL/FAL, Afrikaans HL/FAL, Natural Sciences & Technology, Social Sciences, Life Skills, Life Orientation, EMS, and Technology.

Topics, weeks, hours and assessment splits all come from the official DBE Annual Teaching Plan.

Can I generate resources in Afrikaans?

Yes. Set the Language toggle in the sidebar to Afrikaans. The whole document — questions, instructions, memo and slide deck — will be in Afrikaans, with authentically South African contexts.

Resource types

What's the difference between Worksheet, Test and Exam?
  • Worksheet: 10–30 marks, single section. Practice instrument.
  • Test: 30–75 marks, term-scoped, 2–4 sections.
  • Exam: 50–100 marks. T2 exam covers T1+T2 content; T4 exam covers T3+T4 — that's the CAPS rule.
  • Final Exam: 80–150 marks, full-year scope.

We also have Investigation, Project, Practical, Assignment and Lesson plans (DOCX + PowerPoint).

What's the "Generate full unit series" button?

It's a one-click upsell that generates every lesson in a CAPS unit in sequence — each lesson aware of which subtopics came before, so the series builds on itself.

Same per-call cost as N separate generations; the convenience is the win. The button only appears for Lessons on units with 2+ subtopics.

Can I edit a resource after it's generated?

Yes — two ways:

  1. In the browser: switch the preview to Edit mode and tweak the text inline. Click "Rebuild DOCX" to regenerate the Word file with your changes.
  2. In Word: download the .docx and edit normally. The file isn't locked or watermarked.

You can also use the Refine box to ask the AI to make a single targeted change — "Make question 2 harder", "Add a question on multiplying decimals", etc.

Sign-in & password

I didn't get my sign-in / verification / reset email
  1. Check your spam/junk folder. Mark anything from us as "Not spam" so future emails land in your inbox.
  2. Wait 60 seconds — sometimes there's a queue. We rate-limit ourselves to 3 emails per minute per address to avoid being flagged as a spammer.
  3. Confirm you typed the email correctly on the sign-in form.
  4. Try a different email address (some school networks block external mail).

Still stuck? Email hello@theresourceroom.co.za from the address you tried to sign up with.

The link in my email says "this link has already been used"

That usually means your email provider scanned the link before you clicked it. Gmail's "Safe Links", Outlook's "Defender" and some school proxies pre-fetch every URL in your inbox to check it for malware — and our magic links are single-use, so the scan burns the link.

The fix: request a fresh link from the sign-in page. When the new link arrives, click it, and on the confirmation page click the Sign in button. The button-click step (a POST request) isn't pre-fetched by scanners, so the token survives.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in form, click Forgot password?, enter your email, and we'll send a reset link. The link is valid for 15 minutes.

This same flow also works if you signed up before passwords existed — your account had no password attached, so you'll set one for the first time on the reset page.

What are the password rules?

Minimum 10 characters. We don't force capital letters or symbols — modern guidance prefers length over complexity. Choose a passphrase that's memorable but not obvious.

Your account

How do I update my profile (name, school, grades…)?

Click the Account button in the top-right corner of the app. Edit your details and click Save — your changes appear immediately and follow you across devices.

How do I delete my account?

Click Account in the top-right, scroll to the Danger zone at the bottom, click Delete my account, and type your email to confirm.

Your profile, settings, history and sign-in sessions are removed from our database immediately. We'll send a confirmation email to your address.

Why is my email "unverified"?

When you signed up we sent a verification link to your email. Until you click it, you'll see a yellow banner at the top of the app reminding you. The app still works — but verifying your address means we can reset your password if you ever lose it.

Click Resend link on the banner if the original got lost.

Generated content

Are the questions actually CAPS-aligned?

Yes. Every generation pulls topics, weeks, hours and assessment splits directly from the DBE Annual Teaching Plan, then asks Claude (Anthropic's AI) to fill a strict CAPS-aware schema. The output is then validated against that schema and the marks are rebalanced to add up correctly.

Term-scope rules are enforced automatically — a Term 2 exam can't include topics it shouldn't.

Should I review what's generated before using it?

Yes — every time. AI models can occasionally produce subtly wrong questions, awkward phrasings, or factual mistakes. Treat each output as a strong first draft to review, not a finished product.

The good news: that review takes minutes, not hours, and the structural correctness (mark balance, term scope, schema) is already done.

The generation is taking a long time

Most resources finish in 20–60 seconds. Lessons (which produce a DOCX + PowerPoint pair from a single tool call) and Final Exams (largest schemas) can take up to 2 minutes.

If you're stuck on a phase for more than 3 minutes, click Cancel and try again. If the problem repeats, email us with the time and grade/subject/topic you tried.

Privacy & data

What data do you collect about me?

Only what you choose to share: your email address, your profile (name, school, role, grades you teach — all optional except the email), and the resources you've generated (so we can show you your history). See the full Privacy Policy.

We do not collect learner data, payment cards (Stripe handles those), or sensitive personal information.

Do you train AI models on my data?

No. Generation requests go to Anthropic's Claude API, which doesn't train on API traffic by default. We don't sell, share, or aggregate your data with anyone for AI-training purposes.

Is the app POPIA-compliant?

We're built with POPIA in mind from day one — encrypted in transit (HTTPS), HMAC-signed session cookies, no password storage (we use scrypt hashing), and a one-click account deletion that wipes your data immediately.

The Privacy Policy covers the legal detail. Note: that page is a draft and will be reviewed by a SA legal advisor before we go to production.

Billing

How does the trial work?

Every new account gets a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. Generate as many resources as you like during that time.

When your trial ends, you'll need to choose a paid tier to keep generating. Your existing resources, history and settings are yours forever.

What are the paid tiers?

Pricing is in South African rand and billed monthly or annually (annual is 20% off):

  • Essential — R179/month or R1,719/year
  • Classroom — R349/month or R3,349/year
  • Pro — R599/month or R5,749/year

Each tier has a different monthly generation cap — the Upgrade button in the app shows current details. Cancel any time.

How do I cancel?

Click your tier badge in the top-right and choose Manage billing. That opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you can cancel, change plans, update card details or download invoices.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — no pro rata refunds.
